    BTS (Bangtan Sonyeondan)

    BTS is one of the most globally recognized K-pop groups. They are known for their powerful music, socially conscious lyrics, and incredible stage presence. BTS has a massive international fanbase and has achieved immense success worldwide.

    BLACKPINK

    BLACKPINK is a popular girl group known for their edgy style and catchy songs. They are recognized for their fierce performances and have a significant following both in South Korea and internationally.

    EXO

    EXO is a boy band known for their impressive vocal and dance skills. They have a unique concept of being divided into two subunits, EXO-K and EXO-M, to cater to both Korean and Chinese markets.

    TWICE

    TWICE is a girl group known for their infectious and cheerful music. They are famous for their catchy tunes and colorful music videos, which have garnered them a large fanbase.

    NCT (Neo Culture Technology)

    NCT is a unique group with a rotational concept, meaning they have different subunits and members that can change over time. This concept allows for various musical styles and combinations within the group.

    Red Velvet

    Red Velvet is a girl group known for their versatility, as they can effortlessly switch between different music genres and concepts. They have a distinct and captivating style.

    GOT7

    GOT7 is a boy band known for their energetic performances and diverse discography. They are recognized for their close bond with fans and their involvement in composing and producing their music.

    Mamamoo

    Mamamoo is a girl group known for their powerful vocals and soulful music. They have gained acclaim for their live performances and their ability to harmonize seamlessly.
